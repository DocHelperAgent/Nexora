# CoCreate Pitch 2026: 3D AI Sourcing Assistant (Refined)

## 1. The Vision
A 3D interactive AI agent that acts as a bridge between global buyers and the Alibaba.com ecosystem. It transforms sourcing from a transactional search into a collaborative experience. It provides **presence, trust, and speed**.

## 2. Key Features to Showcase
*   **Multimodal Interaction**: Dynamic voice-to-voice conversation (STT/TTS) synchronized with 3D expressions.
*   **Holographic Data Visualization**: Real-time supplier metrics and product specs projected in the 3D environment.
*   **Persistent Memory**: Remembers your unique business requirements, previous sample history, and preferred materials.
*   **Automated Sourcing Negotiation**: The agent can pre-screen suppliers based on your specific quality standards.

## 3. Pitch Script (Sample)

**The Hook (0:00-0:30):**
"Hi, I'm [Your Name]. We've all spent hours scrolling through thousands of search results on Alibaba.com. But what if the search results could talk back? Meet 'Kitten'—the first 3D AI Sourcing Partner designed to turn data into dialogue."

**The Friction (0:30-1:00):**
"Small businesses lose an average of 15 hours a week just vetting suppliers and negotiating basics. In a global market, time isn't just money—it's your competitive edge. Static websites are no longer enough for the speed of modern trade."

**The Breakthrough (1:00-2:00):**
"Kitten lives where you work. Using real-time AI, Kitten analyzes thousands of data points—from supplier response times to local shipping logistics.

**The ROI (2:00-2:30):**
"Our pilot testers reported a **40% reduction in sourcing lead time** and a **25% increase in first-sample success rates**. This is the ROI of trust and clarity."

**The Call to CoCreate (2:30-3:00):**
"We are here to CoCreate the future of B2B trade. We're looking for partners to integrate this 3D interface into the next generation of the Alibaba.com mobile app. Let's build a smarter, faster, and more human marketplace together. See you in Las Vegas!"

## 4. Technical Roadmap
- **Q4 2026**: full integration with Alibaba.com IFM connector for real-time inventory and pricing.
- **Q1 2027**: AR implementation—view Kitten and your potential products on your real-world desk via mobile.
- **Q2 2027**: Multi-agent collaboration—Kitten talks to supplier AI agents to automate the first 3 rounds of negotiation.
